Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Summary: ProfitCalc Pro does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All processing occurs locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

What This Extension Does

ProfitCalc Pro is a Chrome extension that calculates e-commerce profit margins on product pages from Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, and Walmart. It reads publicly available product data (such as product titles, prices, and categories) from the page you are viewing and displays a profit calculator panel.

Data Collection

ProfitCalc Pro does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No data is transmitted to any external server, API, or third-party service.
  • No browsing history, cookies, or tracking data is collected or stored externally.
  • No account creation, login, or personal identification is required.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking is performed.

Data Processing

All profit calculations and fee computations are performed entirely within your browser using JavaScript. The extension reads publicly displayed product information from the page you are visiting (e.g., price, product title) and processes it locally using built-in fee tables for each platform.

Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally in your browser only (using Chrome's local storage):

  • Your fee preferences (e.g., Shopify plan, billing cycle, fulfillment method)
  • Modifier selections (e.g., apparel, hazmat, oversize)

This data never leaves your browser. It is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. The extension does not make any external network requests, does not connect to any servers, and does not transmit any information to any third-party services.

Third-Party Services

ProfitCalc Pro does not integrate with, embed, or load any third-party services, scripts, or resources. All code is bundled locally within the extension.

Children's Privacy

ProfitCalc Pro does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The extension is intended for use by e-commerce sellers and business professionals.
